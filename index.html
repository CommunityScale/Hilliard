Understanding Hilliard's housing needs and opportunities to support Comprehensive Plan implementation

Hilliard is working to understand its housing market and identify opportunities to support diverse, affordable housing options for current and future residents.

Study goals:

Understand current housing conditions and future needs.
Engage residents and stakeholders to understand community priorities.
Identify housing production targets that meet local needs.
Develop recommendations for policy and planning improvements.
